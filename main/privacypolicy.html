Waves Privacy Policy

At **Waves** (accessible at https://boredomarcade.xyz), we care about your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it to provide you with a fun and secure experience.

1. Information We Collect

  • **Personal Information:** We only collect personal details like your name, email, or phone number if you choose to give them to us voluntarily (e.g., when you sign up or contact us).
  • **Non-Personal Information:** When you visit our site, we automatically gather some non-personal data. This includes your IP address, browser type, which pages you came from, and how long you spend on different parts of our site. This helps us understand how our site is used and how we can make it better.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • **To Improve Your Experience:** We use the information we collect to continually enhance our website's content, features, and overall design.
  • **Advertising:** We partner with third-party advertising providers, including Google, to display ads on our site. Google uses cookies to show you ads based on your visits to Waves and other websites.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

  • **What are Cookies?** Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device to remember your preferences and activities.
  • **Our Use of Cookies:** We use cookies to:
    • Personalize content and ads.
    • Enable social media features.
    • Analyze website traffic.
    You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but be aware that this might affect how our site works for you.

4. Google AdSense

  • **Third-Party Advertising:** Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (specifically DART cookies) to serve ads on Waves. These cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other places on the internet.
  • **Opting Out:** If you'd prefer to opt out of DART cookies, you can do so by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy page.

5. Third-Party Privacy Policies

This Privacy Policy applies only to **Waves**. It doesn't cover other advertisers or third-party websites linked from our site. We recommend you check the privacy policies of any third-party ad servers or external websites you visit.

6. Children's Privacy

Protecting children's privacy is incredibly important to us. Waves does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us right away so we can take appropriate action.
